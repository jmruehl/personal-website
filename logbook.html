Introducing the Scrimba Platform

Written by Merle on July 19, 2021

During the weekend I studied Module 3 'Making websites interactive' of the Scrimba Course. The Module contains 166 videos with a total lenght of 8 hours. In general videos - so called scrims - vary from less than a minute to a few minutes and they are rarely more than 6 minutes long.

Each video contains a specific topic and a task to code by yourself. And here the great advantage of the Scrimba Editor comes into play. You can stop the scrim at any time and the video turns into an editor with the full code avaiable. So at any given moment you can actually code yourself and even save your that code to a note. Isn't that a great way to learn?!
